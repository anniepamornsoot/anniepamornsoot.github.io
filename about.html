Nice to Meet You

Hi There —
I'm Your Guide
to Phuket

I didn't just read about Phuket in a brochure. I packed my life, moved here, and built a home on this island. Seven years later, I've experienced everything — the excitement, the overwhelm, the adjustments, and the pure joy of watching this place become home.

Now I help families like yours do the same, minus the guesswork.

Let me rewind a little...

I wasn't born here. I chose to be here.

Years ago, I made the decision to leave everything familiar behind and start a new chapter in Thailand. No family connections, no company transfer — just a deep feeling that this was where I was meant to be.

Those first months? Exhilarating and terrifying in equal measure. Finding an apartment, opening a bank account, navigating a new language, figuring out which neighbourhood actually suited my lifestyle — I learned it all the hard way.

Then everything changed...

I experienced Phuket as a parent — and it's a completely different world.

Living in Phuket as a single person is one thing. Living here with kids? The questions multiply overnight. Which school has the right curriculum? Is this neighbourhood safe for children? Where will they make friends? Is there a good paediatrician nearby?

I've navigated all of this firsthand. I know which international schools actually deliver on their promises. I know which areas have the best community feel for families. And I know what it's like to worry about whether you're making the right call for your children.

I know what it takes to uproot your life and start over in a place where everything is new. That's exactly why I do what I do.

Here's what makes me different...

I give you facts, not sales pitches. So you can decide with confidence.

I'm not here to sell you a dream or push a listing because of a commission. I'm here because I genuinely believe every family deserves honest, unbiased information when making one of the biggest decisions of their lives.

After 7 years of living, working, and raising a family in Phuket, I've built the kind of local knowledge you can only get from actually being here — not from a website or a weekend visit. I know the hidden costs, the real pros and cons of each area, and the things no one tells you until you've already signed a lease.

My job is to give you all of that, lay it out clearly, and let you make the decision that's right for your family.
