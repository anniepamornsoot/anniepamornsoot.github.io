The overview

A three-bedroom contemporary villa tucked into a quiet residential lane five minutes from Laguna's main gate. The house was built in 2019 and feels it — high ceilings, a flow of sliding doors between the living room and the pool, and just enough storage for a family who actually needs it.

The garden is fully walled and gets good afternoon shade, so kids can be outside without you having to hover. Two of the bedrooms open onto the same hallway — good for siblings, slightly tight if you're planning guests every month.

My honest notes

What I'd tell a friend

Morning light is excellent, afternoon light is good. There's a minor road noise issue from Saturday mornings (local temple market) but nothing on weekdays. The master shower runs cold for the first 30 seconds — owner has been told, fix scheduled. Internet speed tested at 340 Mbps.

The kitchen is smaller than the photos suggest. If you cook daily, you'll probably want a second prep table.

What's inside

Air-con in all rooms Full kitchen, gas hob Washer & dryer Fully furnished Fibre internet Pets negotiable Estate security 24/7 Child-friendly pool fence

The neighbourhood

Laguna is where a lot of expat families start, and for good reason. International schools are within 10 minutes, Bang Tao Beach is a short drive, and the Boat Avenue strip covers most of your weekday needs — supermarkets, cafés, clinics, a cinema. Quiet at night, busy on Sundays.
