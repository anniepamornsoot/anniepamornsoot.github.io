Hi, I'm Esmiranda

I founded Tailandesita because I believe the best way to experience Thailand is through someone who truly lives it. Born and raised near Bangkok, I've spent years uncovering the hidden corners of this country — from secret street food stalls to mountain temples that few tourists ever see.

Every tour is personally led by me. No large groups, no cookie-cutter itineraries. Just authentic experiences tailored to what you want to see, taste, and feel. Whether you're a solo traveler, a couple, or a small group of friends — I'll make sure your Thailand trip is unforgettable.
