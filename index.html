Life in Phuket

Everything You Need to Know
Before You Move

Relocating with family or retiring abroad is a big decision. We've been helping expats navigate Phuket life for nearly two decades — here's what matters most.

International Schools

Phuket is home to over a dozen accredited international schools offering British, American, IB, and Australian curricula — with waiting lists filling fast. We guide families to the right areas for school proximity.

  • BISP (British International School)
  • HeadStart International School
  • QSI International School
  • UWC Thailand

World-Class Healthcare

Phuket's hospitals are among Southeast Asia's finest, with internationally trained staff and JCI-accredited facilities. Peace of mind comes standard when you live here.

  • Bangkok Hospital Phuket
  • Mission Hospital Phuket
  • Vachira Phuket Hospital
  • 24/7 expat clinics

Visas & Residency

From Thailand Elite visas to long-term retirement (Non-OA) and education visas — we connect you with trusted immigration lawyers to make your move seamless and legal.

  • Thailand Elite Visa (5–20 yr)
  • Non-OA Retirement Visa
  • Non-B Work Permit
  • LTR (Long-Term Resident) Visa

Property Ownership

Foreigners can legally own condominiums freehold, or structure villa ownership through leasehold agreements and Thai limited companies. We ensure your investment is fully protected.

  • Condo freehold (up to 49%)
  • 30+30+30 yr leasehold
  • Thai company structures
  • Full due diligence support

The Phuket Lifestyle

Year-round sunshine, world-class dining, sailing, golf, yoga retreats, and a thriving expat community. Phuket is not just a place to live — it's a way of living.

  • 300+ sunny days per year
  • International dining & nightlife
  • Golf courses & marinas
  • Strong expat community

Cost of Living

Compared to Western cities, Phuket offers exceptional value. A comfortable family lifestyle with domestic help, dining out regularly, and private schooling is attainable at a fraction of the cost.

  • Household staff from ฿15,000/mo
  • Fine dining from ฿500/person
  • International school from ฿350,000/yr
  • Private healthcare packages
