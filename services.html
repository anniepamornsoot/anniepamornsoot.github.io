Common Questions

The things everyone asks.

How much does your help cost?

My relocation service is free to you. The property owner pays my fee, and only when we find a home that actually fits — never up-front. Stand-alone services (school shortlist, area tour) are priced on my contact page. No surprises.

Do I have to come to Phuket before signing a lease?

No. Many of my families sign based on video tours alone — I walk every room, record the street, the neighbours, the morning light. If something feels off, I tell you. If I wouldn't rent it myself, I won't show it.

We have kids — can you really help with schools too?

Yes. Schools often come first in the conversation, because the right school changes the right area. I've done the tours, read the curricula, and I know which heads answer emails the same day and which ones don't.

What if we've never been to Phuket before?

Then this is exactly what I'm here for. Many of my families arrive with just a dream and a few Pinterest boards. I slow it down, match you to an area based on how you actually live, and make sure nothing gets rushed.

How long does the whole process take?

Typical families take 6–10 weeks from first call to moving in. Some move faster, some take six months. We go at your pace, not mine.

Can you help if we want to buy, not rent?

I usually recommend renting for the first year so you truly understand the island. If you're still set on buying, I'll intro you to a lawyer I'd send my own family to, and stay involved throughout.
