Quick Answers

Before you write in.

Is there a cost to our first conversation?

No. The first 30-minute call is always free and there's no obligation afterwards. If we don't feel like the right fit, I'll happily point you elsewhere.

How quickly do you usually reply?

Within 24 hours on weekdays, sometimes faster. If you need something urgent, WhatsApp is the best channel.

What timezone do you work in?

I'm on GMT+7 (Phuket). I usually reply to messages between 8am and 7pm local time, and I'm happy to do calls early morning or late evening to suit European or American families.

Do you work with families who aren't moving permanently?

Often. Sabbatical families, gap-year families, 6-month trial relocations — all welcome. The help looks slightly different, but the heart is the same.
